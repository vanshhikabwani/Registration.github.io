PAINTING COMPETITION

Join us for an amazing event!

Register Here

Competition Details:

Eligibility: Students from classes 8 to 12.

Theme: "My Vision for a Drug-Free World"

Format: Create a visually captivating poster on an A3 size sheet that depicts your vision of a drug-free world. Use colors, illustrations, and powerful messages to convey the importance of a drug-free society.

Registration: All participants must register themselves through this link. The registration fee is INR 250 for each participant.

Submission: After registration, all participants have to submit their entry by 22nd June, 2023, to our office address through courier.

FDFWI Office Address: 2/13A Jungpura, Block A, New Delhi - 110014

Note: No entry will be considered after the deadline.


Judging Criteria: Posters will be evaluated based on creativity, originality, clarity of message, and overall visual impact.

Winners Announcement: The winners will be announced on 26th June, 2023, on our official handles and will be contacted individually.

Prize: All participants will receive an e-certificate of participation. Additionally, three winners will be selected from each school. There will be a different prize pool for differently-abled students.

  • 1st Prize: Trophy and Gift Voucher for Rs. 1000
  • 2nd Prize: Trophy and Gift Voucher for Rs. 1200
  • 3rd Prize: Trophy and Gift Voucher for Rs. 1500
